	2.	Total number of instructions = total number of loops * (# of instructions per loop)
	 	10^15 * 10 = 10^16
	3.	CPU spped = 10^9 instructions/second
	4.	Total seconds required = 10^16 / 10^9 = 10^7
	5.	Total days required = 10^7 /(24*3600) = 115.74 days
